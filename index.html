Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Garden: Focus your attention is developed by Diego Magnus. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data — including focus sessions, garden progress, coins, and preferences — is stored locally on your device.

No Account Required

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

Subscriptions

The App offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions ("Garden Pro") managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase
  • Subscription automatically renews unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
